About us

Welcome to Intelligent Unmanned Systems Laboratory! The Intelligent Unmanned Systems Laboratory is dedicated to advancing theoretical exploration and innovative research in frontier areas including networked control, multi-agent systems, learning-based control, and intelligent systems. We place strong emphasis on the integration of theory and practice, and actively promote the application of advanced control theories to the cooperative control of mobile robots, including UAVs and UGVs, as well as soft robots.
